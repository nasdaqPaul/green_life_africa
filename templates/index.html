Green Life Africa

Green, Healthy, Wealthy, Community

Green Life Africa (GLA) is a registered Youth Focused, Community Based Organization (CBO) with a vision of a Green, Healthy, Wealthy community! The organization is located in Machakos town, John Mulwa building 2nd floor, and implements programs in: Environmental Justice, Economic Empowerment, Food Security and Nutrition.
